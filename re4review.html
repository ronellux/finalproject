Resident Evil 4

Resident Evil 4 was developed by Capcom and released on March 29, 2023.
It is a remake of the 2005 orginal game that improves upon the original material in every
single way imaginable.


Updated Graphics
The graphics have been updated using the new RE Engine, which accounts for a visual overhaul that is sure to amaze fans.

Expanded Story
The story has been expanded upon the original to include additional story beats and add more context as to why events are
occuring the way they are. The game features 16 chapters, and they all vary from 20-50 minutes in completion time.

Smoother Gameplay
The gameplay runs extremely smooth compared to the 2005 original, with new abilities such as parrying, running, quickturning, crouching and smaller QOL changes that enhances the user's experience while playing the game.

Conclusion
The remake to Resident Evil 4 no doubt is a worthy upgrade to the already iconic original game, enhancing every aspect of the
original game into a worthy experience. Regardless if you've played the original or not, there is much to be desired here, and with the newly released "Gold Edition" featuring bonus content for the game, this is the perfect time to experience true survival horror.
